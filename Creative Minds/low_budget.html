Andamans Low Budget Package Itinerary Rs.21,999/-(5Days/4Nights)

 

  • Day 1-Arrival at Port Blair
  • Day 2-Port Blair – City Tour and Chidiya Tapu
  • Day 3Port Blair - Havelock
  • Day 4Port Blair – City Tour and Chidiya Tapu
  • Day 5-Port Blair – Departure to hometown

Arrive at Port Blair airport and proceed for your transfer to the hotel. On reaching, check-in to your hotel, unpack and relax. After lunch (on your own) proceed for Viper Island (Harbor Cruise). In the evening visit Cellular Museum and Cellular Jail, which stood mute witness to the torture meted out to the freedom fighters, who were incarcerated in this Jail. History awakens as we visit 'Veer Sawarkar' Smarak and see the light and sound show.

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and transfer to the jetty to board the Govt ferry to Neil Island. Upon arrival, transfer to the hotel and check-in. Set out for a sightseeing trip to Bharatpur beach in the evening. Enjoy a hearty dinner and a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel in Neil Island.Relish a sumptuous dinner and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel in Havelock.

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed to visit Elephant Beach followed by a session of Snorkelling. One may enjoy the underwater beauty through a glass bottom boat and Snorkelling. Return to the hotel in the afternoon. Post lunch, visit one of the best beaches in Asia – Radhanagar Beach. Enjoy dinner and a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.

Today proceed for Port Blair city tour covering the Chatham Saw Mill, Forest Museum, Anthropological Museum, Fisheries (Marine) museum, Naval Marine museum (Samudrika).Later visit Ross Island - the island presently houses ruins of old buildings like Ballroom, ChiefCommissioner's house, Government House, Church, Cemetry, Hospital, Bakery, Press, Swimming Pool and Troop Barracks, all in dilapidated condition, reminiscent of the old British regime

Check-out of the hotel and transfer to the airport. Board the flight for return journey. Tour ends with Memories Unlimited.
