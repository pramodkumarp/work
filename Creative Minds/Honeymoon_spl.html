Andamans Honeymoon Package Itinerary Rs.64,999/- (7Days/6Nights)

 

  • Day 1-Arrival at Port Blair
  • Day 2-Port Blair- Havelock
  • Day 3Havelock-Elephant Beach
  • Day 4-Havelock- Neil Island
  • Day 5-Port Blair -Jolly Buoy Island
  • Day 6-Port Blair – Departure to hometown
  • Day 7-Port Blair – Departure to hometown

Arrive in Port Blair, report to the coach and proceed to hotel. In the evening visit Cellular Museum and Cellular Jail, which stood mute witness to the torture meted out to the freedom fighters, who were incarcerated in this Jail. History awakens as we visit 'Veer Sawarkar' Smarak and see the light and sound show.

Visit Ross Island - the island presently houses ruins of old buildings like Ballroom, Chief Commissioner's house, Government House, Church, Cemetry, Hospital, Bakery, Press, Swimming Pool and Troop Barracks, all in dilapidated condition, reminiscent of the old British regime. Later enjoy Paddle boat ride at water sports complex. Visit Chidiyatapu is the southern most tip of South Andaman. The lush green mangroves, forest cover with numerous chirping birds.

Proceed to Havelock island by M V Makruzz cruise. Visit Radhanagar Beach - rated as Asia's best beach in 2004 by the Times magazine. The island is known as one of the jewels of the Andaman archipelago

Visit Elephant Beach , one of the most popular beaches in Havelock Island Known for its beautiful corals and exotic marine life. Enjoy Snorkelling and also you can enjoy various activities on your own like Scuba Diving,Jet Ski, Sofa Ride, Banana ride, Speed boat ride.

Day free to enjoy the blue water of Havelock. Enjoy boat ride with a glass of Wine in the evening. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening.

Visit Kalapathar beach, lined stretches of golden sand, and surrounded aquamarine water, developed into its own style of island paradise later enjoy Mangrove creek tour lush with tropical forest. Proceed to Port Blair by M V Makruzz cruise. Free time for relaxation in the evening

Check-out of the hotel and transfer to the airport. Board the flight for return journey. Tour ends with Memories Unlimited.
