Andamans Premium Package Itinerary Rs.34,999/- (6Days/5Nights)

 

  • Day 1-Arrival at Port Blair
  • Day 2-Port Blair- Havelock
  • Day 3Havelock-Elephant Beach
  • Day 4-Havelock- Neil Island
  • Day 5-Port Blair -Jolly Buoy Island
  • Day 6-Port Blair – Departure to hometown

Board your flight for Port Blair from your hub (DELHI/ MUMBAI/KOLKATTA/ CHENNAI) You will be met upon arrival by Easy Haven Holiday representative who will transfer you to your hotel in Port Blair. Upon arrival, proceed for check-in at your hotel. Post Lunch (on your own) visit Corbyn's Cove Beach. Thereafter proceed to Anthropological Museum and Aquarium .Return to the hotel late in the evening .Enjoy dinner and a comfortable overnight stay at the 4 Star hotel in Port Blair.

After breakfast , checkout from the Hotel to visit Ross Island. Customers who miss the visit to Ross Island on this day due to closure will be taken to Cellular Jail. Later board the ferry for the journey to Havelock Island. Upon arrival, proceed for check-in at your hotel. Enjoy dinner and a comfortable overnight with Beach Party.

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed to visit Elephant Beach followed by a session of Snorkelling. One may enjoy the underwater beauty through a glass bottom boat and Snorkelling. Return to the hotel in the afternoon. Post lunch, visit one of the best beaches in Asia – Radhanagar Beach. Enjoy dinner and a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.

After buffet breakfast, check out from the hotel. Transfer to jetty to board the ferry to Neil Island. On arrival at Neil, proceed for check in at your hotel. Later proceed to Laxmanpur Beach or Bharatpur Beach. Enjoy dinner and a overnight stay at the hotel.

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Board your ferry back to Port Blair. In the evening, visit Sagarekha for shopping. Enjoy Makruzz/Coastal Cruise dinner and Boat Party.

Check-out of the hotel and transfer to the airport. Board the flight for return journey. Tour ends with Memories Unlimited.
