Arrive in Port Blair, report to the coach and proceed to hotel. In the evening visit Cellular Museum and Cellular Jail.History awakens as we visit 'Veer Sawarkar' Smarak and see the light and sound show.

Visit Neil Island - , the beautiful island with lush green forest and sandy beach. Today We also have a short Meet n’ Greet session before dinner.

Proceed to Havelock island by M V Makruzz cruise. Later we visit Radhanagar Beach – rated as Asia’s best beach in 2004 by the Times magazine. The island is known as ‘one of the jewels of the Andaman

Visit Elephant Beach , one of the most popular beaches in Havelock Island Known for its beautiful corals and exotic marine life.

Welcome to Port Blair by M V Makruzz cruise / Costal Cruise. Today we explore the city of Port Blair where we visit Chatham Saw Mill – one of the largest mills in Asia, Cottage Industries Emporium and Forest Museum that offers an insight into forest activities through scale models and displays decorative pieces made of famous woods like Padauk, Marble, Peauma, Gurjan, Satin Wood, etc.

Today we visit Jolly Buoy Island. You can enjoy various water sports activities on your own and swimming in the crystal clear blue water. Explore the Coral formations by the glass bottom boat ride. Free time for leisure activities in the evening. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening.

It’s time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through email, Facebook…. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all!!! ‘I Fly Tour’ guests will proceed to airport as per group flight schedule. Joining and Leaving Tour’ guests will proceed to airport as per their flight schedule. Tour concludes.
